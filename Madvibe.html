About us
The Madvibe dance crew was founded in September 2013.It was
established with the hope of hyping the spirit of hip-hop in Eucharistic
Heart of Jesus Model College.Our goal is to make sure that we serve
and entertain you to the maximum. This crew started with few guys
who felt that there is the need for its existense. We are into chore-
ography and frestyle dancing; we are always availbale for hiring.
We dance for the fun and have fun because we dance.
